⛰ Trip Overview

A road trip from Rangsit to Khao Kho (เขาค้อ), the "Switzerland of Thailand" in Phetchabun Province. Known for its misty mountain views, sea of clouds at sunrise, and cool breezes, Khao Kho sits at 800+ meters elevation and offers a refreshing escape from Bangkok's heat.

💡 Midway Stop Option: The drive is approximately 5 hours. We may stay overnight midway (around Nakhon Sawan area, ~2.5 hours from Rangsit) on the way there and/or on the way back.

April Weather in Khao Kho

Expect warm days around 33°C with cooler mountain nights at ~22°C. April marks the start of the rainy season, so pack a rain jacket. The elevation provides relief from the heat compared to Bangkok.

🌟 Top Attractions

Temple

Wat Pha Sorn Kaew

Stunning hilltop temple with 5 giant white Buddha statues surrounded by mountains and colorful mosaic pagodas. One of Thailand's most beautiful temples.

Wind Farm

Khao Kho Wind Farm

100-meter tall windmills with strawberry and cabbage farms below. Take a 60 THB tram ride for a close-up view of the turbines.

Viewpoint

Phu Tub Berk

At 1,768m elevation, this is the prime spot for camping, stargazing, and watching the sunrise over a sea of clouds.

Viewpoint

Khao Kho Post Office

Famous sunrise viewpoint with panoramic sea of clouds views. Has a camping area for overnight stays.

Nature

Sri Dit Waterfall

A lovely waterfall with shaded walking paths and historical relics scattered along the trail.

Garden

Khao Kho Flower Terrace

Cascading terraces of flower fields with a skywalk for aerial views and a cozy mountain cafe.

🚗 Driving Route

Rangsit → Khao Kho

  1. Start from Rangsit, head north on Highway 1 (Phahonyothin Rd)
  2. Continue to Nakhon Sawan (~2.5 hrs)
  3. Take Highway 117 toward Phitsanulok
  4. Then Highway 12 (Phitsanulok-Lom Sak)
  5. Follow signs to Khao Kho
Midway Option: Nakhon Sawan (~halfway) is a good spot for an overnight rest stop if the group wants to break up the drive.

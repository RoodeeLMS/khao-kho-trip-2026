🚗 Driving Tips

Route Tips

  • Leave Rangsit by 6:00 AM to avoid Bangkok traffic
  • Highway 1 north is the main route — straightforward
  • The last stretch into Khao Kho has winding mountain roads
  • Download Google Maps offline before departure
  • Fill up gas before entering the mountain area

Safety

  • Rotate drivers on long stretches
  • Mountain roads can be foggy in the morning
  • Watch for motorcycles on mountain curves
  • Keep headlights on in the mountains
  • April rain showers can make roads slippery

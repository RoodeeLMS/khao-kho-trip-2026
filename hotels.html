🍴 Where to Eat in Khao Kho

We have a full kitchen & BBQ at the villa, but these are worth going out for:

☕ Cafes

  • Pino Latte Resort & Cafe — Beautiful mountain cafe on Road 12. Sea of fog views, bakery, food & drinks
  • Jolly Cafe Khao Kho — Country farm cafe, Western menu, great desserts. English & Thai menu
  • Le Bonheur — Quiet cafe with cakes, pasta, sandwiches. Peaceful atmosphere
  • Route 12 Coffee — Popular local coffee shop along the main road

🍴 Restaurants

  • Moai @ Khao Kho — Greenhouse-style brunch spot. T-bone steak, pork steak. Very popular
  • Rai Chan Raem — Good Thai food, amazing passion fruit juice, beautiful flower gardens
  • Khao Kho Tavern — Chinese-Thai fusion in a mountain setting
💡 Tip: Buy groceries & meat in Phitsanulok or Lom Sak on the way up for BBQ nights at the villa!

🚗 Food Stops Along the Route

Nakhon Sawan (~2.5 hrs)

Riverside city — great for a food break

🍴 Food Stop
  • Pad Thai Mae Payom — 50-year-old legendary pad thai, crispy wontons & secret sauce
  • Krua Ta Nam Oi — Riverside restaurant, affordable Thai food, river views
  • Moka Cafe — Famous premium mini croissants, great coffee

Phitsanulok (~3.5 hrs)

Famous night market & riverside dining

🍴 Food Stop
  • Food Night Market — THE must-visit! Som tam, grilled pork, crab curry, crepes, every evening
  • Tontan Restaurant — In Grand Riverside Hotel, Thai + fusion, riverside
  • Fakerah — Fresh roti grilled on a hot plate with homemade curries
💡 Grocery Stop: Phitsanulok has big supermarkets (Big C, Tesco Lotus). Stock up on meat, snacks & drinks for the villa BBQ before heading up the mountain.

Nakhon Sawan is a major city at the halfway point with plenty of hotel options, restaurants, and gas stations. A good place to break up the 5-hour drive.

🔍 What to Look For

  • Budget-friendly hotel for 1 night
  • Near Highway 1 for easy access
  • Parking for the car
  • Rooms for 5 people (2-3 rooms)
  • Close to restaurants for dinner/breakfast
📝 Decision Needed: Do we want to stay midway on the way there, on the way back, both, or neither? This depends on how early we want to depart and how tired the group is after driving.

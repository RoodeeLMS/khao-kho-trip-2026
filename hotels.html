🍴 Food: The 250-seat restaurant serves Thai & Western dishes. Breakfast buffet included. Reviews mention good salads, Thai spring rolls, and curried pork roti. The food is affordable, though the buffet breakfast could have more variety day-to-day.
🏠 Group Fit: The Cotswold duplex villas have 2 bedrooms on separate levels — perfect for 5 people. The resort grounds are huge with gardens, a maze, and a pool to keep everyone entertained without leaving.

🚗 Midway Stop Hotels

Nakhon Sawan (~2.5 hrs from Rangsit)

Riverside city with great local food

💡 Midway Option

🏨 Best Hotel: 42C The Chic Hotel

Category4-star
PoolYes
SpaFull-service spa — Thai massage, deep tissue, sports massage
LocationCity center, Nakhon Sawan

Other options: P.A. Ville Hotel (3-star, on-site restaurant) or B2 Nakhon Sawan (budget).

🍴 Where to Eat

  • Pad Thai Mae Payom — 50-year-old legendary pad thai with crispy wontons & secret sauce
  • Krua Ta Nam Oi — Riverside restaurant, affordable & delicious Thai food, river views
  • Moka Cafe — Famous premium mini croissants, great coffee
  • Kalosu — Cute Korean restaurant with indoor & outdoor seating

Phitsanulok (~3.5 hrs from Rangsit)

Closer to Khao Kho — famous night market & riverside dining

💡 Midway Option

🏨 Best Hotels

Topland HotelCentral location, restaurant with live band, from ~$31/night
Grand RiversideRiver views, Tontan Restaurant on-site (highly rated Thai & fusion)
Poonsook HotelBudget option from ~$20/night
💡 Closer to Khao Kho: Only ~1.5 hours from Phitsanulok to Khao Kho, making it a great last-stop before the mountains. Better for the return trip since you'll pass through it on the way down.

🍴 Where to Eat

  • Food Night Market — THE must-visit! Som tam, grilled pork, crab curry stir-fry, crepes, Chinese doughnuts every evening
  • Tontan Restaurant — In Grand Riverside Hotel, authentic Thai + fusion, riverside setting
  • Nannum Restaurant — Top-rated Thai cuisine, well-balanced flavors, serene setting
  • Fakerah — Watch fresh roti grilled on a hot plate, pair with homemade curries

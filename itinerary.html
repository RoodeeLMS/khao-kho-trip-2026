Day 1 — Road Trip & Settle In

Wednesday, April 22
▼
06:00

Depart from Rangsit

Early start to beat the traffic. Head north on Highway 1 (Phahonyothin Rd). Snacks ready!

🚗 Driving
08:30

Nakhon Sawan — Breakfast Decision Point

Option A — Quick stop: Grab breakfast at Pad Thai Mae Payom (legendary 50-year-old pad thai) or Moka Cafe (famous mini croissants), then continue driving.
Option B — Stay overnight: Check into 42C The Chic Hotel (4-star, pool, spa). Explore the riverside, eat at Krua Ta Nam Oi (river views, affordable Thai food). Continue to Khao Kho tomorrow morning.

🍴 Food
💡 If continuing straight to Khao Kho:
11:00

Check in at Zentony Pool Villa

Arrive at Zentony Pool Villa in Thung Samo. Unpack, pick your bedrooms, check out the pool & mountain views.

🏨 Check-in
12:00

Lunch

Cook something at the villa kitchen, or head to a nearby restaurant. Take it easy — no rush to go anywhere.

🍴 Food
14:00

Pool & Chill

Swim in our private saltwater pool, play pool table, or just nap. This is your time to decompress from the drive.

🌴 Relax
16:30

Afternoon Coffee at Pino Latte If We Feel Like It

Short drive to this beautiful mountain cafe near Wat Pha Sorn Kaew. Sea of fog views, bakery, great drinks. Or just stay at the hotel!

🍴 Cafe
18:30

BBQ Dinner & Karaoke Night

Fire up the BBQ at the villa! Or go out to Moai @ Khao Kho (great steaks) or Jolly Cafe (Western menu). Either way, end the night with karaoke at the villa.

🍴 Food

Day 2 — Resort Day & Good Food

Thursday, April 23
▼
08:00

Sleep In & Breakfast

No alarm needed. Wake up naturally. Make breakfast in the villa kitchen or grab something from a nearby cafe.

🍴 Food
09:30

Morning at the Villa

Pool, pool table, or just hang out on the terrace. Read a book, chat, play cards. Enjoy the mountain air and windmill views.

🌴 Relax
11:30

Brunch / Early Lunch Off-site Option

Cook at the villa, or drive to Moai @ Khao Kho for their popular brunch (T-bone steak, pork steak in a greenhouse setting).

🍴 Food
13:30

Casual Outing If We Feel Like It

Pick one easy activity:
• Wat Pha Sorn Kaew — Stunning temple, 15 min drive
• Flower Terrace — Skywalk + mountain cafe
• Wind Farm drive-through — Scenic, stay in the car
• Or just stay at the hotel!

🌟 Optional
15:30

Cafe Hopping / Coffee Time

Try Jolly Cafe (farm-style, Western menu, great desserts), Le Bonheur (quiet, cakes & pasta), or Route 12 Coffee.

🍴 Cafe
17:00

Back to Villa — Golden Hour

Enjoy the sunset from the villa. Pool, pool table, or just chill on the terrace with the windmill & mountain views.

🌴 Relax
19:00

Dinner

BBQ night #2 at the villa, or try a different restaurant. Karaoke rematch! Khao Kho has fresh mountain vegetables and local Phetchabun specialties.

🍴 Food

Day 3 — Check Out & Head Back

Friday, April 24
▼
08:00

Last Breakfast at the Villa

Make one last breakfast in the kitchen. No rush — soak in the morning mountain air by the pool.

🍴 Food
10:00

Check Out & Depart

Pack up, clean up the villa, check out, and begin the drive south. Gas up before leaving the mountain area.

🏨 Check-out
12:00

Phitsanulok Lunch Decision Point

Option A — Lunch stop: Eat at Tontan Restaurant (Grand Riverside Hotel, Thai fusion) or grab Fakerah roti & curry, then continue to Rangsit (arrive ~16:00).
Option B — Stay overnight: Check into Topland Hotel (central, live band restaurant) or Grand Riverside Hotel. Hit the famous Food Night Market for dinner — som tam, grilled pork, crab curry, crepes!

🍴 Food
13:00

Nakhon Sawan Alternative Or Stop Here Instead

If skipping Phitsanulok, stop in Nakhon Sawan for lunch at Krua Ta Nam Oi (riverside Thai food) and stay at 42C The Chic Hotel (pool, spa, Thai massage).

🍴 Food
16:00

Arrive Home (If Driving Straight)

Back in Rangsit by late afternoon!

💡 If staying midway: Add a Day 4 for a relaxed morning drive back. Breakfast at the hotel, depart ~09:00, home by ~11:30 (from Nakhon Sawan) or ~12:30 (from Phitsanulok).

Day 4 — Easy Drive Home If Staying Midway

Saturday, April 25 (Optional)

Hotel Breakfast

Relaxed 2.5-3.5 hour drive back. No rush, stop whenever you want!

🚗 Driving
12:00

Arrive Home

Back in Rangsit by noon, well-rested and well-fed. Trip complete!

Day 1 — Hit the Road

Wednesday, April 22
▼
06:00

Depart from Rangsit

Early start to beat the traffic. Head north on Highway 1 (Phahonyothin Rd). Make sure the car is fueled up and snacks are packed!

🚗 Driving
08:00

Breakfast Stop

Stop for breakfast at a roadside restaurant or gas station along Highway 1. Stretch your legs!

🍴 Food
08:30

Nakhon Sawan Area Decision Point

Option A: Continue driving straight to Khao Kho (arrive ~11:00)
Option B: Stop and explore Nakhon Sawan, check into a hotel, rest, and continue tomorrow morning

💡 If continuing to Khao Kho (Option A):
11:00

Arrive at Khao Kho

Check in at The Blue Sky Resort. Freshen up and settle into your villa.

🏨 Check-in
12:00

Lunch at Blue Sky Restaurant

Enjoy lunch at the resort's 250-seat restaurant with mountain views.

🍴 Food
14:00

Explore the Resort

Wander through the flower garden, hedge maze, and enjoy the pool. The resort grounds are huge with European-style architecture.

🌴 Leisure
16:30

Khao Kho Viewpoint — Sunset

Drive to the Khao Kho viewpoint for a panoramic sunset over the mountains and valleys.

🌟 Sightseeing
18:30

Dinner

Dinner at a local Khao Kho restaurant or back at the resort. Try local Phetchabun dishes!

🍴 Food

Day 2 — Temples & Mountains

Thursday, April 23
▼
05:00

Sunrise at Phu Tub Berk Optional

Wake up early and drive to Phu Tub Berk (1,768m) for a spectacular sunrise over a sea of clouds. Worth the early wake-up!

🌟 Sunrise
07:30

Breakfast

Return to the resort or find a local cafe for breakfast.

🍴 Food
09:00

Wat Pha Sorn Kaew

Visit the iconic hilltop temple with 5 giant white Buddha statues and stunning mosaic art. Allow 1.5-2 hours to explore the grounds. Wear comfortable shoes for the steps!

🌟 Must-See
11:30

Khao Kho Wind Farm

See the massive 100m windmills up close. Take the tram ride (60 THB) and visit the strawberry farms below. Great photo spot!

🌟 Sightseeing
12:30

Lunch

Lunch at a mountain restaurant near the wind farm area. Fresh vegetables from local farms!

🍴 Food
14:00

Khao Kho Flower Terrace

Walk through cascading flower terraces, take the skywalk for aerial views, and relax at the mountain cafe.

🌴 Nature
15:30

Weapon Museum Optional

Learn about Khao Kho's Cold War history. The area saw significant conflict and this museum tells the story of reconciliation.

🌟 History
17:00

Resort Pool & Relax

Head back to Blue Sky Resort. Enjoy the pool, gardens, or just relax with mountain views.

🌴 Leisure
19:00

Dinner

Group dinner at the resort or a recommended local restaurant.

🍴 Food

Day 3 — Waterfall & Return

Friday, April 24
▼
07:00

Breakfast & Check-out

Enjoy a final breakfast at the resort. Pack up and check out of Blue Sky Resort.

🏨 Check-out
08:30

Sri Dit Waterfall

Visit this lovely waterfall with shaded walking paths. A refreshing morning activity before the drive back.

🌴 Nature
10:00

Depart Khao Kho

Begin the drive back toward Rangsit via the same route. Stop for snacks and fuel.

🚗 Driving
12:30

Lunch Stop Decision Point

Option A: Quick lunch stop along the highway, continue to Rangsit (arrive ~15:00)
Option B: Stop for lunch in Nakhon Sawan area, check into a hotel, and drive back to Rangsit the next morning

🍴 Food
15:00

Arrive Home (Option A)

Back in Rangsit! Unpack, rest, and reminisce about the mountain views.

💡 If staying midway (Option B): Add a Day 4 for the final 2.5-hour drive from Nakhon Sawan to Rangsit in the morning, arriving fresh and rested.

Day 4 — Return Day If Staying Midway

Saturday, April 25 (Optional)
▼

Breakfast in Nakhon Sawan

Enjoy breakfast at the hotel or a local morning market.

🍴 Food

Depart for Rangsit

Relaxed 2.5-hour drive back to Rangsit. No rush!

🚗 Driving
11:30

Arrive Home

Back in Rangsit by lunchtime, well-rested from the midway stop. Trip complete!
